The Best Movies According to Franklin

My top 5 movies of all time.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (Both Parts)

This is an amazing movie. Really shows what happened in the book.

The Wandering Earth 2

Great sci-fi movie! Really realistic and shows many environmental issues (although it is a bit hard to understand)

The Martian

Another great sci-fi! This really shows what would happen if an astronaut got stuck on Mars. Great visual to the book!

Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (Play)

This is an amazing sequel to the Harry Potter series. Really nice! Illustrates time travel too!

Transformers

This is the best of all in my opinion. The adventure is really suspensful.
